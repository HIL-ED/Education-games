Western Front · 1915 – 1918

You are the engineering officer of a front-line sector. Every spade of earth, coil of wire, and gun position is a decision. Design your trenches, defend them against bombardment, gas, and assault — and discover why the trenches of the Great War took the strange, zig-zagging, layered shapes they did.

A classroom simulation · outcomes reflect your engineering, not dice.
