A US History Review Game

US History Connections

Inspired by the popular puzzle: find groups of four items that share a hidden historical connection. Ten eras to choose from, from the Revolution to the Civil Rights Movement.

How to play

You'll see 16 people, places, events, and terms in a grid. They secretly belong to four groups of four. Select four tiles you think belong together, then hit Submit.

Difficulty colors

Once you solve a group, it's revealed in one of four colors, from most straightforward to trickiest:

Straightforward Solid Recall Tricky Trickiest

Mistakes

You get 4 mistakes before the puzzle ends. If you select four tiles and only three belong together, you'll see a "One away..." hint. Watch out for terms that seem to fit more than one group; that overlap is the whole game.

Tools

Shuffle rearranges the remaining tiles if the grid gets confusing. Deselect All clears your current picks.
